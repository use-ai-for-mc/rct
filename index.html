Calibrate me. Tap the button the instant the Good car pulls out of GotG. The first tap locks the phase to real life; every tap after that also sharpens the cycle period — the longer your taps span (an hour → ±0.1 s, a day → ±0.02 s), the slower it drifts. A single hardcoded period can't stay accurate: it's only known to ±0.2 s, which over many cycles accumulates real drift, and a server restart resets the cycle entirely — so it has to be anchored to live sightings, just like the in-game mod. The two cars run half a cycle apart and are identical, so which one is “Good” can't be derived from the anchor — if the labels land on the wrong cars, hit ⇄ swap A/B once.
